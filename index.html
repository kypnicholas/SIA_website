    How listing verification works

    The "Data verified" badge is generated from automated internal checks to improve listing reliability. It is not a legal certification.

    • Field completeness: ID, title, size, price, status and image are present.
    • Format and plausibility: area, price and unit-price values are checked for valid ranges.
    • Status and title deed consistency checks are applied.
    • Location sanity checks validate coordinate format and expected geographic bounds.
    • Contact details are validated for basic email and phone format.

    Final due diligence remains with the buyer. We can provide supporting documents and map references on request.
